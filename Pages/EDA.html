Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is an approach to analyzing data that involves summarizing and visualizing the main characteristics of the data in order to gain insights and identify patterns. To perform EDA on Twitter data related to supplement intake, you could start by collecting a dataset of tweets that mention supplements. You could use a Twitter API to search for tweets containing keywords related to supplements, or you could use a pre-existing dataset of tweets that have been collected and labeled for sentiment analysis. Once you have your dataset, you could start by cleaning and preprocessing the data to remove any irrelevant information or noise, such as retweets or spam messages.
